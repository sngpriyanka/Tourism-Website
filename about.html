Our Story

Born in the Shadows of the Himalayas

Yatra Nepal was founded in 2014 by a group of passionate Nepali trekkers, guides, and cultural enthusiasts who believed that Nepal deserved to be experienced — not just visited.

The word "Yatra" (यात्रा) means journey or pilgrimage in Nepali. Every trip we design is a yatra — a meaningful journey that connects you with Nepal's soul.

Today, we've guided over 10,000 yatris from 50+ countries through Nepal's most incredible landscapes, always keeping sustainability and authenticity at the heart of everything we do.
