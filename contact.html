FAQs

Common Questions

Most nationalities can get a visa on arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport. Tourist visas are available for 15, 30, or 90 days. Indian nationals don't need a visa.

October-November (autumn) offers the best trekking weather. March-May (spring) is great for rhododendron blooms. Monsoon (June-August) is ideal for rafting.

Our prices are listed in USD for international travelers' convenience. We accept USD, major credit cards, and bank transfers. Local expenses in Nepal use Nepalese Rupees (NPR).

It depends on the trek. Poon Hill is suitable for beginners, while Everest Base Camp requires moderate fitness. We offer treks for all levels.

We strongly recommend travel insurance with helicopter evacuation coverage for treks above 3,000m. Insurance is mandatory for high-altitude treks.
